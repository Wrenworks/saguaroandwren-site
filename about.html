About Saguaro & Wren

Saguaro & Wren is a comic strip rooted in the strange and enchanting rhythms of the Sonoran Desert. The series blends dry humor, heartfelt character moments, and surreal adventures that reflect the harsh beauty of the desert and the bonds that grow within it. Each strip balances whimsy with reflection, exploring resilience, companionship, and the occasional tumbleweed chaos.

The Setting

The strip is set in the Sonoran Desert, a landscape filled with extremes: scorching summers, vibrant flora, and resilient wildlife. Known for its iconic saguaro cactus and breathtaking sunsets, the Sonoran is a land of survival and beauty. Its contrasts mirror the humor and struggles of our characters, making it the perfect backdrop for their adventures. Learn more about the region at Saguaro National Park.

About the Author

Ricardo Porven is the writer and artist behind Saguaro & Wren. A veteran of the independent comics scene, Ricardo is best known as the creator of Donnie Goth, a cult-favorite series that explored gothic subculture through sharp humor and vivid characters.

His career has spanned decades of storytelling, and his work has been featured in interviews with The Webcomic Overlook and ShockYa, where he shared insights into his creative process and passion for visual storytelling.

Through Saguaro & Wren, Ricardo brings his sharp humor, eye for character, and love of surreal desert imagery into a strip that is as thoughtful as it is entertaining.
