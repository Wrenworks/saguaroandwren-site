About Saguaro & Wren

Saguaro & Wren is a comic strip set in the vast beauty of the Sonoran Desert, where a towering cactus and a sharp-witted bird navigate the humor, struggles, and unexpected companionships of desert life. Through humor and reflection, the strip explores friendship, survival, and the odd poetry of the natural world.

The Setting

The strip takes place in the Sonoran Desert, a living landscape spanning parts of Arizona, California, and Mexico. It is one of the most biodiverse deserts in the world, home not only to saguaros and wrens but also to coyotes, jackrabbits, rattlesnakes, and blooming desert wildflowers. For more on this remarkable landscape, visit Saguaro National Park.

About the Author

Ricardo Porven is the creator, author, and artist behind Saguaro & Wren. He is also well known as the author and artist of Donnie Goth, a cult-favorite webcomic that blends gothic humor with sharp character-driven storytelling.

With years of experience in independent comics, Ricardo has built a reputation for bold artistic style, atmospheric settings, and quirky characters. His works have been featured in interviews on The Webcomic Overlook and Shockya, where he discussed his inspirations and creative journey.
