About Saguaro & Wren

Meet the Characters

Wren

Wren is the endlessly curious desert bird who brings humor and heart to the Sonoran Desert. Whether he's juggling mindfulness, chasing shade, or dodging dust storms, his perspective keeps things light and playful.

Saguaro

Saguaro is the tall, steady counterpart to Wren. Rooted in the desert sands, he often provides the dry wit (and sometimes dry wisdom) in the duo’s adventures. Life may be prickly, but he’s seen it all.

The Setting

The stories of Saguaro & Wren unfold in the heart of the Sonoran Desert — a place of stunning beauty and extremes. Home to the majestic saguaro cactus, this desert spans parts of Arizona, California, and Mexico. It’s one of the most biodiverse deserts in the world, alive with unique plants, animals, and vibrant sunsets.

Want to experience it yourself? Visit Saguaro National Park.

About the Author

Ricardo Porven is the creator, writer, and artist of Saguaro & Wren. A longtime cartoonist and storyteller, his work explores humor, identity, and resilience through unique characters and settings. He is also the author and artist of Donnie Goth, a gothic webcomic that gained a devoted following for its wit and originality.

Ricardo has been featured in publications and interviews such as The Webcomic Overlook and ShockYa, where he discusses his creative process and influences.
