About the Author

Ricardo Porven is the creator, author, and artist behind Saguaro & Wren. With a background in comics, illustration, and storytelling, Ricardo has been bringing characters to life for years. He is also the author and artist of Donnie Goth, a gothic slice-of-life comic that blends humor with dark whimsy.

His work has been featured in interviews and articles, including The Webcomic Overlook and Shockya.
